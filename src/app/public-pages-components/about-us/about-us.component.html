About Us

E-Commerce it is an emerging online shopping and selling hub of Pakistani E-Commerce. A subsidiary of E-Commerce Chemicals Group Pakistan, E-Commerce has the potential to become the ultimate online shopping destination for people of Pakistan and elsewhere. With availability of 4000 SKUs at present , E-Commerce plans to move the number of SKUs to millions. Customers can buy, sell and register themselves as vendors at E-Commerce. E-Commerce is working to make your dreams come true by ensuring availability of range of accessories at your doorstep.
